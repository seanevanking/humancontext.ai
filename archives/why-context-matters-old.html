The Challenge

Most businesses struggle to make AI work because their critical business context is trapped in fragmented systems and processes. Traditional approaches force you to replace existing systems, risking the loss of valuable business context.

Our Approach

We create a unified context layer that preserves your business knowledge while making it accessible to AI. This means you can:

  • Keep your existing systems and processes
  • Make your content immediately more findable
  • Build a foundation for AI capabilities
  • Scale intelligence as you grow
