Why Context Matters

Your business knowledge is your competitive advantage. Don't lose it in the rush to implement AI.

The Challenge

Common AI Implementation Pitfalls

Most businesses struggle to make AI work because their critical business context is trapped in fragmented systems and processes. Traditional approaches force you to replace existing systems, risking the loss of valuable business context that has been built up over years.

  • Loss of institutional knowledge during system replacement
  • Disconnected data silos limiting AI effectiveness
  • Inability to preserve business rules and workflows
  • High costs and disruption of complete system overhauls
