Customer-Based Context Engineering

Not everything is broken. But most organizations don't have a reliable way to understand how customers experience the business across systems, teams, and touchpoints.

That's what I bring: a structured way to identify, clarify, and apply customer context across your internal platforms and processes. This helps you see what's supporting the business, what's getting in the way, and what needs to change.

This approach is called customer-based context engineering, a practical methodology for mapping how value is created, experienced, and delivered.
